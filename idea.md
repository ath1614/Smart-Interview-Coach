1. Project Title:
Smart Interview Coach – AI-Powered Interview Practice App

2. Your Name & Roll Number:
Atharv Soni – [Your Roll Number]

3. Problem Statement:
Job seekers often lack access to real-time, personalized interview feedback, making it difficult to improve communication skills and confidence. Traditional mock interviews require scheduling with professionals, which can be expensive and time-consuming.

4. Proposed Solution / Idea:
A React Native mobile app that allows users to practice interviews anytime, anywhere. The app records responses, transcribes speech to text, analyzes tone, and provides AI-generated feedback on clarity, vocabulary, confidence, and filler words. It also suggests personalized improvements and follow-up questions to simulate a real interview experience.

5. Key Features:

Voice and video interview recording

Speech-to-text transcription

AI-powered tone and confidence analysis

Real-time feedback with improvement suggestions

Dynamic follow-up questions for realistic interview flow

Progress tracking over time

6. Target Users / Audience:
Job seekers, students preparing for placements, professionals seeking career growth.

7. Technology Stack:

React Native – Mobile app framework

Expo Camera – Video/Audio recording

Vosk / Coqui STT – Free speech-to-text

Hugging Face Models – Tone & sentiment analysis

AsyncStorage – Local data storage

8. Expected Outcome:
A mobile app that enables users to practice interviews anytime, receiving AI-driven feedback within seconds, and tracking improvement over time without any subscription fees.

9. Timeline:

Week 1–2: Research & UI design

Week 3–4: Core recording & transcription feature

Week 5–6: AI feedback system integration

Week 7: Testing & deployment

10. Additional Notes:
Will use free and open-source AI models to ensure zero recurring costs for users.
